PADDING

An element's padding is the space between its content and its border. padding can be given in point,pixel,centimeter and we can use padding top,left,right,bottom. padding: 25px 50px; top and bottom paddings are 25px right and left paddings are 50px

BORDER

a border that goes around the padding and content

MARGIN

can set the margin property to auto to horizontally center the element within its container. The element will then take up the specified width, and the remaining space will be split equally between the left and right margins.

margin: 25px 50px 75px 100px; top margin is 25px right margin is 50px bottom margin is 75px left margin is 100px

OUTLINE

An outline is a line that is drawn around elements, OUTSIDE the borders, to make the element "stand out".
